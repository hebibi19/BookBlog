1. The Raven King - Maggie Stiefvater



new | rating (10/10)

review: every single time i have to review one of these books i literally never know how to go about it because they can’t be explained. all i can really talk about each time is the characters and how beautifully written their relationships are. also,, i just really love ronan,, idk what it is about him he reminds me of some other character that i cant quite name, its just AH! u know? theres one line from this book specifically that has stuck by me for years because i saw it a long time ago in a tiktok and i didnt understand what it meant, but that line is “adam lived in an apartment located above the office of St.Agnes Cathloic Church, a fortuitious combination that focused most of the objects of Ronan’s worship into one downtown block”,,,, LIKE!!! OH MY GODDD,,,,,, anyway i love how everyone was written and while adam wasnt always my favorite i did love him as much as i loved everyone else,, im sad its over and i cant wait until i can reread them and catch new things i couldnt catch the first time i read them :)

2. The Final Empire - Brandon Sanderson



reread | rating (9.5/10)

review: this book really attacked my daddy issues. like it emotionally devastated me. as far as first books in a series go, I think this book was amazing. was it an extremely slow and difficult start? yes,, i found it hard to really engage myself in with the story and it was just so much information about the maguc system and the world around them that for almost half the book, you don’t even know how you feel about the characters because you havent really been told much about them. despite that! i think that the magic system is really cool and everything else about the world is really cool and i didnt think id be able to understand it all, but as you keep going it just get easier u know. the plot, i feel wasn’t really there,, like they had an end goal and there were all these steps that they were taking to achieve their goal (bc it was like a heist type mission) but i feel as if the book focused a lot more of the characters establishing them and their dynamics. (which personally, I love) now the characters! the book was a dual POV switching back and forth between the two main characters vin and kell (which first of all, love dual pov),,, secondly,, i love vin with all my heart like all i want for her is to be happy,, kell,, we had a rocky patch but i still reallly loved him. and the dyanmic between the two,, oh my god beautful. overall, i really liked it and i thought the writing flowed really well,,, i have absolutely no idea how the rest of the story is gnna go, so im scared for that,, but also excited.

3. The Well of Ascension - Brandon Sanderson



new | rating (8/10)

review: overall, the book was okay,,, it definitely does not match up with the first book which i thought was amazing. it was captivating and entertaining and interesting in all aspects. that could have been because of all of the tropes,, it was a heist and it was a branch of found family and it had a couple of aspects of that enemies to lovers,,, but this book was different. obviously, i knew that there wasnt going to be a heist,, and that the romance was nonexistent, but i at least expected a little more. I cant say that the book wasnt entertainign bc it was, it definitelyt was that... but its hard to claim that it was entertaining when the first book obviously did a much better job at that. there were certain aspects of this book that made me keep going and it definitely was not the dynamic between the two main characters,,, after a couple hundred pages, it gets a little repetitive when their thinking is exactly the same about how they r egard each other,,,three side characters were my favorites (tensoon, spook, zane),, i thought one of them was going to be more important than he actually was,, the other has disappeared but i hope he comes back,, and the third got so little screentime but i relished it when it happened. this book wouldnt pass the bechdel test,,, LMAOOOOO neither would the first one honestly,,, is it bc brandy doesnt know how to write women,, ,,,the book was extremely politicly focused,, there was a war,, so it was a lot of war planning and battle scenes and government meetings,,, none of which interest me, but at least brandy sandy had the decency to put interesting scenes in between political ones. am i excited for the last one? yes, i love the main characters (elend and vin), im interested in how one of them is going to do something specifically,, and honeslty i just love vin so anythign she does ill b cool w,, for a moment there i thought she was gnna have a villain arc tbh,, she did not,, but it wouldve been cool,,, im just hoping she doesnt have to die at the end bc i feel like its something she’d do.

4. The Hero of Ages - Brandon Sanderson



new | rating (8.5/10)

review: at this point of the series i am fully invested in the characters,, i loved tensoon and vin and spook and elend (even tho i always joked about how they should kill him haha,,,, ) ,,, i am mad at brandy,,, why did he have to kill vin (sobbing meme),,,,, and i fr thought that he was gnna kill spook,,, like if spook died i wouldve stopped reading 100%,,,, i also thought tensoon was going to have a much larger role to play and i also thought he was gnna live!! all the kandra died!! that was so sad,,, i dont think anyone had an extremely sad death so i cant be extrememly sad about that (but maybe it wouldve been nice to include some sad deaths),,, i thought this book was def better than the last one and it was def more fast paced and obviously had more riding on it than the second book,,,, there were a few moments where brandy was putting together all of the hints for me but i was wayyy to stupid to actually get them so when they were revealed i was like :0,,,,, overall i think id give the series like a 8/10 or like a 7.9/10,,, just bc i didnt feel completely emotionally attached to as many characters as I would like to have been,,, also,, i guess i kinda just know when a series becomes a favorite, and while i could see the first book (and maybe the last book) def being up there,, im not completely sure they make it,,,, i knew immeadiately with the raven boys and the foxhole court and atyd,,, and as much as i would love to throw mistborn in with them, it just doesnt quite make it,,, sad, but true.

All the Young Dudes

MissKingBean

(fanfiction) this one ruined me. completely entranced by the marauders. ive read these books 3 times in the span of 2 months. i had to physically stop myself from rereading them again.

1. All the Young Dudes - MissKingBean



reread | rating (10/10)

4. Clockwork Princess - Cassandra Clare



reread | rating (10/10)

review: clockwork princess is the best book out of the three undeniably. well, im a sucker for second books in trilogies. it tends to be the book where almost nothing happens but theres a lot of character growth and i love that. but clockwork princess is special in its own regard bc of everything that happens in it. i love magnus and his connection to the shadowhunters and i love the lightwoods and i love when i first found out that brother zachariah was jem! oh my god i rememebr how shocked i was and it was amazing and it literally was a plot twist i never expected like it was crazy to know that i had been reading about him for 5 books and i never noticed. and then when city of heavenly fire came out and jem was in one of the final chapters as himself, ugh that was beautiful.

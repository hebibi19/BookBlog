1 - 6. The Mortal Instruments - Cassandra Clare



reread | rating overall (7.8/10)

review: this series was more pleasant that I had expected it to be. this year I decided that I was going to reread all of cassandra clare's shadowhunter books, and unfortunately, this included the mortal instruemnts. The "are we siblings or not" romance plotline was very much not appreciated, however the secondary characters are really what made the books so nice to read. I absolutely adore simon and magnus and isabelle and alec and was honestly just reading for them at one point. Book 4 focuses most of its attention on Simon (love) and it also is where you begin to notice that the writing has improved into the cassie clare style we know today. the final book was still able to shock me like it did the first time when i read it in 8th grade and for that it deserves some love.

tags: fantasy, shadowhunter-universe, romance, adventure, questionable-plot-for-the-first-four-books, love-magnus-bane, love-simon
